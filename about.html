About Us

Rockheads is a popular indie-rock band from Kathmandu, Nepal, known for their energetic performances and emotionally resonant original music. Formed in 2010 as a cover band playing rock classics in Thamel’s live music bars, they gradually transitioned into creating their own songs.

With three platinum albums and countless sold-out shows across the globe, ROCKHEADS has established themselves as one of the most exciting bands of this generation. Their unique blend of classic rock influences with modern alternative sounds has earned them critical acclaim and a devoted fanbase.
